References & Sources

CareMate's vaccine recommendations are derived exclusively from peer-reviewed guidelines and official public health authorities.

PAPDI, Satgas Imunisasi Dewasa
Indonesian Society of Internal Medicine · 2025
Jadwal Imunisasi Dewasa, Satgas Imunisasi Dewasa PAPDI 2025
Official adult immunization schedule for Indonesia. Primary source for condition-based recommendations including Herpes Zoster, RSV, Influenza, and Pneumococcal as priority vaccines for adults with chronic conditions.
Herpes ZosterRSVInfluenzaPneumococcal
Buku Saku Vaksinasi Dewasa, PAPDI 2025
Pocket reference guide for adult vaccination in Indonesia. Provides clinical context, dosing schedules, and contraindications for all recommended adult vaccines including newly approved RSV and recombinant Herpes Zoster vaccines.
Clinical ReferenceDosingContraindications
Kartu Vaksinasi Dewasa, PAPDI 2025
Adult vaccination card identifying the four vaccines prioritized for adults with comorbidities: Herpes Zoster (Recombinant), RSV, Influenza, and Pneumococcal. Basis for the "Vaccines to Prioritise Now" section in CareMate's output.
Priority VaccinesComorbidities
CDC, Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP)
U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention · 2022–2024
ACIP Recommended Adult Immunization Schedule, United States, 2024
Annual age- and condition-based vaccination schedule covering all recommended vaccines for adults 19 years and older. Used for routine scheduling logic and catch-up recommendations.
Adult ScheduleAge GroupsCatch-up
ACIP Recommendations, Herpes Zoster Vaccine (Shingrix), 2022
Guidelines recommending recombinant zoster vaccine for adults ≥50 and immunocompromised adults ≥19. Informed the age-based and condition-based zoster recommendation thresholds in CareMate.
Herpes ZosterImmunocompromised
ACIP Recommendations, RSV Vaccine for Older Adults, 2023
First ACIP recommendations for RSV vaccines in adults ≥60 and high-risk younger adults. Used to define RSV eligibility criteria in CareMate's recommendation engine.
RSVAdults ≥60
WHO, World Health Organization
Position Papers & Global Vaccine Action Plan · 2012–2024
WHO Position Paper, Influenza Vaccines, 2023
Global recommendations for influenza vaccine use in adults, including priority groups and annual revaccination rationale. Basis for influenza as a universal annual priority in CareMate.
InfluenzaAnnual
WHO Position Paper, Herpes Zoster Vaccines, 2014 (updated 2022)
Global guidance on zoster vaccine use, safety in immunocompromised patients, and priority populations. Supports PAPDI's condition-based recommendations in CareMate.
Herpes ZosterSafety
WHO Position Paper, Pneumococcal Vaccines, 2019
Guidance for PCV use in older adults and high-risk groups. Used to define pneumococcal eligibility for adults with diabetes, lung disease, heart disease, and immunocompromising conditions.
PneumococcalHigh-risk Adults
WHO Position Paper, RSV Vaccines, 2024
First WHO global guidance on RSV vaccines for adults, providing evidence base for RSV as a priority vaccine in adults with underlying conditions.
RSVNew Recommendation
Kemenkes RI, Ministry of Health Indonesia
National Immunization Program & Health Regulations · 2021–2024
Program Imunisasi Nasional Dewasa, Kemenkes RI, 2023
Indonesian national immunization program guidelines for adults, including locally relevant vaccines and schedules adapted to Indonesia's epidemiological context.
IndonesiaNational Program
BPOM RI, Vaccine Authorization & Safety Surveillance, 2024
Indonesian Food and Drug Authority authorizations for COVID-19, RSV, and Herpes Zoster vaccines in Indonesia. Ensures all vaccines recommended by CareMate are locally approved.
AuthorizationSafety
