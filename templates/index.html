Official adult immunization schedule for Indonesia. Primary source for condition-based recommendations including Herpes Zoster, RSV, Influenza, and Pneumococcal as priority vaccines for adults with chronic conditions.

Annual age- and condition-based vaccination schedule covering all recommended vaccines for adults 19 years and older. Used for routine scheduling logic and catch-up recommendations.

Guidelines recommending recombinant zoster vaccine for adults ≥50 and immunocompromised adults ≥19. Informed the age-based and condition-based zoster recommendation thresholds in CareMate.

Global recommendations for influenza vaccine use in adults, including priority groups and annual revaccination rationale. Basis for influenza as a universal annual priority in CareMate.

Global guidance on zoster vaccine use, safety in immunocompromised patients, and priority populations. Supports PAPDI's condition-based recommendations in CareMate.
