Three things in here:
1. How to record the animated reel (`showcase.html`)
2. A scene-by-scene script for a screen recording of the **live site**
3. Ready-to-post LinkedIn copy (mission angle)

---

## 1. Record the animated reel

The file `showcase.html` is a self-running ~42-second reel in CareMate branding.

**To record it:**
1. Open `showcase.html` in Chrome (double-click it, or go to `http://localhost:8090/showcase.html` if serving locally).
2. Press **F11** (Windows) or **Ctrl+Cmd+F** (Mac) for fullscreen — the reel is designed for 16:9.
3. Record your screen:
   - **Mac:** `Cmd+Shift+5` → Record Selected/Entire Screen
   - **Windows:** `Win+G` (Xbox Game Bar) or Loom/OBS
4. The reel auto-advances through 8 scenes and shows a **↺ Replay** button at the end.
   - Press **→ / ←** to step scenes manually, **R** to restart.
5. Trim the recording to start at scene 1 and end on the `mycaremate.me →` close.

**Tip:** add quiet background music in any editor (CapCut, iMovie) — upbeat, soft. Keep it under the spoken/caption pace.

## 2. Screen-recording script for the LIVE site (mycaremate.me)

If you'd rather show the real product, record yourself clicking through. ~60–75 seconds.
Each beat = what's on screen + the caption to overlay (or say).

| # | Show on screen | Caption / voiceover | ~sec |
|---|----------------|---------------------|------|
| 1 | Landing page hero scrolling | "Most people only see a doctor once they're already sick. CareMate flips that." | 0–6 |
| 2 | Start the health assessment, answer 2–3 questions | "A 2-minute assessment scores your prevention health…" | 6–16 |
| 3 | Prevention score result + recommended vaccines | "…and tells you exactly which vaccines and screenings you need." | 16–24 |
| 4 | Dashboard → Lab Results → upload a lab photo | "Snap a photo of any lab report — AI reads the values instantly." | 24–34 |
| 5 | Flagged HbA1c HIGH + recommendations panel | "It flags what's off, then builds your next steps: follow-up tests and the vaccines your conditions call for." | 34–46 |
| 6 | Click "Talk to Doctor" → live AI video consult | "Then talk it through with a live AI doctor — your results already loaded in." | 46–58 |
| 7 | Doctor speaking in the video call | (let 3–4 sec of the real doctor talking play with sound) | 58–64 |
| 8 | Cut to logo + URL | "Prevention, for everyone. mycaremate.me" | 64–70 |

**Recording tips**
- Use a clean browser window, hide bookmarks bar, zoom 100%.
- Pre-load a demo account that already has a flagged HbA1c so the panel is populated.
- Record at 1080p; for LinkedIn, a 1:1 square or 4:5 portrait crop performs best in-feed — but 16:9 is fine.
- Keep each click deliberate; pause ~1s after each screen so viewers can read.

```
Prevention shouldn't be a luxury or an afterthought. It should be one tap away.
```

```
Healthcare in Indonesia is mostly reactive. You get sick, then you go.

The problem isn't that people don't care about prevention — it's that prevention is confusing, scattered across clinics, and hard to access.

So I built CareMate.

→ A 2-minute AI assessment that scores your prevention health and explains what raises it

The goal is simple: help people get ahead of disease instead of just reacting to it.

This is live now at mycaremate.me — I'd love your feedback.

#HealthTech #AI #PreventiveHealth #Indonesia #DigitalHealth #ProductBuilding
```

### Option C — one-liner hook for the video caption
```
What if prevention was as easy as posting on social media? 🩺

Meet CareMate — AI that scores your health, reads your labs, and connects you to a live AI doctor. Built for Indonesia.

mycaremate.me

#HealthTech #AI #PreventiveHealth #Indonesia
```

**Posting tips**
- Lead with the stat or the one-liner — LinkedIn truncates after ~3 lines, so the hook must land first.
- Upload the video natively (don't link to YouTube) — native video gets far more reach.
- Put `mycaremate.me` in the first comment too (links in-post sometimes suppress reach).
- Post Tue–Thu, ~8–10am local time.
- Reply to every early comment in the first hour to boost the algorithm.
